\[X^T{\bf y}=\begin{bmatrix}6\\25\end{bmatrix}\]

\[(X^TX)^{-1}=\frac{1}{40}\begin{bmatrix}46&-12\\ -12&4\end{bmatrix}\]

\[(X^TX)^{-1}X^T{\bf y}=\frac{1}{40}\begin{bmatrix}46&-12\\ -12&4\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}6\\25\end{bmatrix}=\frac{1}{40}\begin{bmatrix}-24\\28\end{bmatrix}\]

So, the least squares line is \(y=-\frac{24}{40}+\frac{28}{40}x=-\frac{3}{5}+\frac{7}{10}x.\)
