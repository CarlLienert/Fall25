8.

\(AX=I\) so \(X=A^{-1}\)

\(AY=0\) Since \(A\) is invertible, the only solution to this equation is \(Y=0\)

Finally, \(AZ+B=0\), so \(AZ=-B\) and \(Z=-A^{-1}B\). (Be careful, make sure you multiplied on the left by \(A^{-1}\))

10.

Start with the middle row times the first column:

\(C+Z=0\), so \(Z=-C\)

The bottom row times the middle column gives \(B+Y=0\), so \(Y=-B\).

Finally, bottom row times the first column gives \(A+BZ+X=0.\) Substitute \(Z=-C\): \[A-BC+X=0\] so \(X=BC-A\).

17.

Multiply the expression on the right:

\(\begin{bmatrix}I & 0\\X & I\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}A_{11} & 0\\0 & S\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}I & Y\\0 & I\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}A_{11} & 0\\XA_{11} & S\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}I & Y\\0 & I\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}A_{11} & A_{11}Y\\XA_{11} & XA_{11}Y+S\end{bmatrix}\)

So: \(A_{12}=A_{11}Y\), and \(Y=A_{11}^{-1}A_{12}\)

\(A_{21}=XA_{11}\), and \(X=A_{21}A_{11}^{-1}\)

Check the bottom right entry:

\[\begin{align*} XA_{11}Y+S&=(A_{21}A_{11}^{-1})A_{11}(A_{11}^{-1}A_{12})+A_{22}-A_{21}A_{11}^{-1}A_{21}\\ &=A_{21}A_{11}^{-1}A_{12}+A_{22}-A_{21}A_{11}^{-1}A_{21}\\ &=A_{22} \end{align*}\]
