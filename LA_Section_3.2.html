37.

If \(A\) is invertible, we can write \[AA^{-1}=I.\]

Take the determinant of both sides:

\[\text{det}(AA^{-1})=\text{det}(A)\text{det}(A^{-1})=\text{det}(I)=1.\]

So, \[\text{det}(A^{-1})=\frac{1}{\text{det}(A)}\]
